1. Donnie Darko (2001)

This one is weird but I love it. It's about a kid who keeps seeing this guy in a rabbit suit named Frank who tells him the world is going to end. The whole movie has a creepy vibe and the ending kind of messes with your head. I've watched it a bunch of times.

back to top

2. The Departed (2006)

Scorsese movie with Leo DiCaprio and Matt Damon. One of them is a cop pretending to be a criminal and the other is a criminal pretending to be a cop. The whole movie is them trying to figure out who the other guy is. Jack Nicholson is in it too. It's stressful in a good way.

back to top

3. Marriage Story (2019)

This one is sad. It's about a couple getting divorced and you see it from both of their sides. Adam Driver and Scarlett Johansson are both really good in it. There's a scene where they have a huge fight in an apartment that is kind of hard to watch but it's so well done.

back to top

4. Pirates of the Caribbean (2003)

The first one is the best one. Johnny Depp as Jack Sparrow is just really funny and the movie is fun the whole way through. I have probably seen this movie more times than any other movie on this list. Hans Zimmer's music is amazing too.
